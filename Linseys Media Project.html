Quote:

“And over time, the wear and tear of loneliness may have very noticeable effects on general well-being.” (Miller, 2015, p.238)

Explination:

In this song, the singer is begging for God to find him somebody to love. He is a lonely man and emotionally isolation is destroying him. He is experiencing problems physically (e.g. trouble standing) and emotionally (e.g. constant crying). He’s also experiencing social isolation because his friends think he’s crazy so he doesn’t feel like he can count on them. This song shows how the stress of loneliness can take a toll on one’s body and mind.
